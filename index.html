how it’s made?

The secret of ice cream from "Icecream" is the use of only natural products and their balanced combination. The production of ice cream for children comes with a reduced amount of sugar, and in ice cream and other desserts for those who follow their figure, it is completely excluded.

We purchase fresh milk, cream and butter and thoroughly test them in our internationally certified laboratory. Thanks to this, we can confidently say that we care about the quality of our products. That's why our ice cream is so delicious!

Our ice cream is the healthiest!

Manufacturing process

  • Fold and mix the mixture
  • Pasteurization of the mixture
  • Filtration
  • Homogenization of the mixture
  • Cooling and ripening (at least 4 hours)
  • Freezing - whipping and partial freezing
  • Tempering of ice cream at t -40℃
  • Ice cream packaging
